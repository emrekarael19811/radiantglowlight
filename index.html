Inspiration

Scenes that help shoppers imagine the product in their own home.

Use lifestyle imagery on the website to build desire. Keep close-ups and utility shots for Etsy detail views. That difference is what makes the site feel like a real brand instead of a duplicated shop page.

Best homepage imagery

Use hero images that feel lived in.

For the homepage, the strongest images are pendant clusters above a kitchen island, desk lamps in warm studies and wall sconces in refined interiors. These immediately signal style, mood and quality.

Best Etsy imagery

Keep Etsy for detail, variety and conversion.

On Etsy, include more technical angles, close-ups, dimensions and color variations. On the website, lead with atmosphere and cohesion. That separation lets both channels do their job better.
