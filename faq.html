FAQ

Common questions about how the website works, where orders are placed and why the showroom is designed differently from an Etsy listing.

Can I buy directly on this website?

The website is a curated showroom. Every product card links directly to the corresponding Etsy listing, where checkout is completed.

Why not show prices on the website?

Keeping the website price-free helps it feel more premium and editorial. Etsy remains the transaction layer, while the website focuses on trust, inspiration and brand identity.

Why are the images here different from Etsy?

The website uses stronger lifestyle images to communicate mood and style. Etsy can then focus on conversion imagery such as close-ups, technical views and variation details.

Is this package ready for Netlify?

Yes. The zip includes static HTML, CSS, assets, sitemap, robots.txt, a Netlify config and redirect files.

Can more products be added later?

Yes. New cards can be added easily by duplicating the existing product card structure and linking to the new Etsy listing.
